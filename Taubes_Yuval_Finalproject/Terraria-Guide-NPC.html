Terraria Guide to NPC's

Non-Playable Characters (NPC) are characters within Terraria that can sell items, heal you, and provide information. This article will take you through the basics of Terraria NPC’s.

There are always two conditions to meet for an NPC to move in.

  1. You have completed the condition for the specific NPC
  2. You have an open house available for the NPC
Once these two conditions have been met the NPC will move into an open house. When the NPC has moved in it will give you a notification at the bottom left-hand side of the screen.

Each NPC has its own requirement to meet before it moves into your world. It can be as simple as defeating a boss, or as difficult as collecting all the other NPC’s. Each NPC also needs a house. A house has to include:

  • A table
  • A chair
  • Light Source
  • Completed Walls

An example house for an NPC

Each NPC Has its own Happiness rating, Happiness can lower or raise prices, and make the NPC sell a pylon. Each NPC has its own favourite biome and People. Refer to this page on the Terraria wiki for more information.

This was the Basic Guide to Terraria NPC’s, You should now be able to include Towns inside your Terraria worlds. Good Luck Terrarian.
